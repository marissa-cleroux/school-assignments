History

The idea came to the founder one cold night long ago. She was enamored by Socrates and his voracity for debate - until his fateful end. This sort of end was inevitable to come to her as well; being the disobedient woman she was at a time not fully equipped to handle her. Before her unfortunate death she figured she couldn't be the only one seeking to challenge and be challenged. She started the Devil's Advocate Club, which has evolved to its current state.

Team Members

Ester

Ester is our foremost debater. She will dissect your argument and quite possibly change your worldview. She works well with almost any debate topic. An extravagent polymath, with an open mind, can take any side in any argument. The true Devil's Advocate.

Abacus

Abacus is the best for simple contradiction. He will take any debate you have and simply contradict you. There is no being right with Abacus. If you just want to be told your wrong this is the debater for you.

Spike

Spike has always been the kind to think outside of the box. His arguments will simply leave you speechless. Don't let his zany appearance frighten you, just wait for his arguments.

Oliver

Oliver has opinions and he makes them loud. Another popular debater, he often enjoys coming from the scientific stand point. One of our top researchers for well laid out arguments.
